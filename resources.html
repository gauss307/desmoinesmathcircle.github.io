Author: Maximillian Roach

Learn about logarithms by checking out this DMMC handout. It contains:
  • An introduction to logarithms.

  • Solutions to numerous example problems.

Introductory Counting Handout
Author: Maximillian Roach
November 2, 2024
Begin your journey through the exciting world of combinatorics with this handout! It contains:
  • The Fundamental Principle of Counting.
  • Factorial notation and overcounting.
  • Essential counting techniques, such as complementary counting, constructive counting, and casework.
  • A selection of challenging exercises.
  • Over 20 worked-out examples.

Divisors Handout
Author: Maximillian Roach
November 7, 2024
Discover the importances of divisors with this handout! It contains:
  • An introduction to primes and prime factorization.
  • How to find the LCM and GCD of two numbers.
  • Derivation of functions that find the product, sum, and number of divisors of a number.
  • A selection of related exercises from popular Canadian and American competitions.
